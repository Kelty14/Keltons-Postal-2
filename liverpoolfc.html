Liverpool FC

Liverpool FC is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England, competing in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. The club has a storied history, having been established following a dispute between Everton FC and Anfield stadium’s owner, John Houlding. Liverpool played its first match in 1892 and quickly became a significant force in English football.

Liverpool Facts

  • Liverpool FC was founded in 1892 after a dispute between Everton FC and Anfield’s owner, John Houlding
  • Liverpool have won 6 UEFA Champions League titles, the most by any English club
  • The club’s anthem, “You’ll Never Walk Alone,” originated from the musical Carousel and became iconic at Anfield in the 1960s.
  • They completed a historic treble in 2001, winning the FA Cup, League Cup, and UEFA Cup

Titles in Champions League

Liverpool FC has won the UEFA Champions League a total of six times. The titles were secured in the following years: 1977, 1978, 1981, 1984, 2005, and 2019. This achievement places them joint third on the all-time winners list.
