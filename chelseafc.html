Chelsea FC

Chelsea Football Club (Chelsea FC) is a professional football club based in Fulham, London, founded in 1905, known for its rich history and significant achievements in both domestic and international football

Chelsea Facts

  • Chelsea FC are one of five clubs to have won all three pre-1999 main European club competitions, and the only club to have won all three major European competitions twice
  • Chelsea won their first major trophy, the League Championship, in the 1954-55 season
  • Frank Lampard, Chelsea's all-time top scorer, netted 211 goals for the club from 2001 to 2014.
  • Chelsea became the first English club to spend over £100 million on transfers in a single season (2003-04).

Titles in Champions League

Chelsea Football Club has enjoyed significant success in the UEFA Champions League, winning the competition twice: in the 2011–2012 season, when they defeated Bayern Munich in the final, and again in the 2020–2021 season, with a victory over Manchester City.
